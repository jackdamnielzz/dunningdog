- Owner: Founder / Product Marketing
- Status: Draft v1
- Last Reviewed: 2026-02-17
- Linked ADRs: [ADR-0001](../adr/ADR-0001-tech-stack.md), [ADR-0003](../adr/ADR-0003-job-orchestration.md)

## Phase 1: Validation (Weeks 1-2)
1. Build-in-public posts and founder outreach.
2. Waitlist capture with clear value proposition.
3. Recruit 10 beta users with Stripe test/live readiness.

## Phase 2: Private Beta (Weeks 3-4)
1. Onboard first 10 users.
2. Validate dunning flow outcomes and user setup time.
3. Collect testimonials and recovery metrics.

## Phase 3: Public Launch (Month 2)
1. Product Hunt launch Tuesday 12:01 AM PST.
2. Cross-post to Reddit, IndieHackers, and X.
3. Publish launch case study with real recovery data.

## Phase 4: Post-Launch Growth (Months 3-6)
1. SEO content on failed-payment recovery.
2. Lead magnet calculator for involuntary churn loss.
3. Referral incentive for customer-led distribution.

## Launch Readiness Checklist
1. Core recovery flows tested end-to-end.
2. Pricing page and onboarding path finalized.
3. Incident and support playbooks ready.
4. Monitoring and alerting configured.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Each launch phase has measurable targets and owner accountability.
2. Launch materials reflect product scope and claims accurately.

## Non-Goals
1. Paid acquisition heavy strategy at MVP launch.
2. Multi-language launch assets at MVP stage.
